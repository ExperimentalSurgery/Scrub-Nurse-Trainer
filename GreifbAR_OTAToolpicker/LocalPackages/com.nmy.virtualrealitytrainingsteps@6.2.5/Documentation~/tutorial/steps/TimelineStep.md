# TimelineStep

 A training step that plays a timeline on a Playable Director.

<p align="center">
    <img alt="TimelineStep.png" src="../../images/steps/TimelineStep.png"/>
</p>

## Usage

Use this step to play a complex animation sequence as timeline. 
It waits until the [Playable Director](#playable-director) is finished playing.

## Parameters

### Playable Director

A reference to a ``PlayableDirector`` that will be played by this step.
