# ChapterTrainingStep

A training step representing a chapter in a virtual reality training sequence.

<p align="center">
    <img alt="ChapterTrainingStep.png" src="../../images/steps/ChapterTrainingStep.png"/>
</p>

## Usage

Use this step for structuring the step sequence in logical parts. Add all sequences that belongs to this 
chapter to the ``Next Steps`` list. Alternatively press ``Alt+A`` to inject all children into the list.
